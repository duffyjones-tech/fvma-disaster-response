Members click their unique link to speak with the AI response agent
FVMA Disaster Response Agent
A conversational AI that speaks with each member in a natural, caring call. It already knows their name and practice. All responses are logged automatically — no forms, no typing, just talking.
Questions the agent asks every member
  • 1
    Are you personally safe and unharmed right now?
  • 2
    Can you confirm the current address and best phone number for your practice?
  • 3
    How many veterinarians do you currently employ at your practice?
  • 4
    Are all of your veterinarians safe and accounted for right now?
  • 5
    Is your practice currently operational and open to patients?
  • 6
    If not — what is the main reason? Power outage, flooding, facility damage, evacuation, or something else?
  • 7
    What is the best way for the FVMA to reach you right now?
  • 8
    Is there anything specific the FVMA can do to help you or your practice right now?
Sample member experience
Agent: “Hi Dr. Williams, this is Jordan calling from the Florida Veterinary Medical Association. We just want to make sure you and your team are doing okay after the hurricane. Do you have a couple of minutes?”
Dr. Williams: “Yes, I'm safe, thanks for calling.”
Agent: “So glad to hear that. Is your practice at 892 Gulf Blvd still your current location, and is that phone number still good?”
Dr. Williams: “Yes, same address. But we're closed — we lost power and had about two feet of flooding.”
Agent: “I'm so sorry to hear that. Is there anything the FVMA can do to help you right now?”
Dr. Williams: “If you could help us find a generator rental that would be amazing. We have some ICU animals that need power.”
